The Begin of Our History

It all started in 2018, when Eduardo Silva, at that time a programming student, started to create his first projects to test his skills as a form of entertainment. From these first projects his family noticed his great skill in creating websites and advised him to follow this path. At the time, his wife had a small cake business, so he saw the perfect opportunity to test his skills by building her a website. However, after finishing the website, he felt that something was missing and wasn´t entirely satisfied by how the application he had created fell outside his expectations. He decided to find out why he had been so dissatisfied with his project and, after spending 15 hours studying day after day, he realized his lack of satisfaction had stemmed from trying to do everything himself and so the project ended up not meeting his expectations. And so, this led him to invite his friends, fellow students of design, programming and management, to join him. And just a few months later, ES Web Developers was born!

Eduardo Silva
